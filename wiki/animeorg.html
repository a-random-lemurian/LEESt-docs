Anime Organization

The Anime Organization is an archival organization active in the Republic responsible for the preservation of primarily two types of entertainment: paper-based still animations called "manga" and movies or videos whose frames consisted primarily of animated frames called "anime".

Originally founded by elements of an old organization named the Internet Archive which through it's ancient "Wayback Machine", the foundation of the web archives of the Republic Libraries would preserve over 1.44 trillion websites on the World Wide Web before it grew into the Worlds Wide Web, the Anime Organization freely distributed in the 2300s copies of 21st century animes such as "Demon Slayer", "Bakemonogatari", and "Sword Art Online", whose copyright had long expired.

The Anime Organization is the ancestor of the Sayari Plushie Authority.
